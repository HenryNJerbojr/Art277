ABOUT THE PROGRAM

The primary goal of the Young Leaders Conference is to empower young people globally with leadership skills to engage in discussions that affect their specific regions and provide the necessary tools and means to identify and understand community issues around them.

The Young Leaders Conference-North America will welcome young people between the age of 18-26 from North America who are motivated to expand their leadership knowledge and engage in important discussions that advance their region’s well-being.

Objectives of the Young Leaders Conference-North America:
Reach more young people in North America and equip them with leadership skills and increased awareness of social justice; Increase knowledge in identifying and understating social justice for the participating youth; Provide mentorship and financial support to develop community projects. Image The Young Leaders Conference Over a span of three days, the conference’s sessions will include: redefining leadership, personal leadership building, engaging with communities, and developing soft skills. The conference will focus on creating a safe environment where participants can engage in discussions about social justice and social entrepreneurship.
